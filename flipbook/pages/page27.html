Story Time
Sit around your teacher and listen to the story, Barry, the Bat*.
Look at the pictures. Colour the one that matches the story.
Talk Time
1. Tell your teacher what Barry was afraid of.
2. What did his mother do to make him feel less afraid?
3. Do you feel afraid at night?
Note to the teacher
* You could explain that bats hang upside down and sleep during the day and that bats go out
at night to look for food.
