Miss Worm
Under a web, beside a gate,
Spider hangs, his legs are eight.
Above him flies Buzzy Bee,
Six black and furry legs has she.
Cat goes running past,
Her four legs carry her so fast.
I’ve only two that isn’t many.
But Miss Worm she hasn’t any!
Listen and repeat the poem after
your teacher.
