Shrubs and Herbs
Shrubs
Note to the teacher
Children can be taken to the school garden and shown the various herbs and shrubs growing
there. The difference between a ‘shrub’ and ‘herb’ should also be explained to them. The fact
that most of the ’herbs‘ are aromatic and soft-stemmed must be explained.
